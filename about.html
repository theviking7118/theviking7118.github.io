About Me

Duck Generator is a fun and playful web application that allows you to generate and view images of ducks in various poses and situations. You can choose from a variety of pre-defined ducks or generate a random one to add some quacktastic fun to your day!

This is a simple project created to demonstrate basic web development concepts like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Feel free to explore the different duck options and have some fun!
